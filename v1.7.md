# v1.7 — Product Quick View

Read PROJECT_CONTEXT.md.

Continue from the current project.

Build ONLY v1.7.

## Goal

Replace the current "Add to Cart" behaviour with a Quick View panel.

Do not redesign the website.

Reuse the existing cart system.

---

## Behaviour

When the user clicks "Add to Cart"

Open a modal (desktop) or bottom sheet (mobile).

Do NOT add the item immediately.

---

## Product Panel

Display

- Product image (placeholder if unavailable)
- Product name
- Description
- Category
- Price

---

## Pizza

Require the customer to select

- Small
- Medium
- Large

Update the displayed price instantly.

Do not allow adding without selecting a size.

---

## Other Products

Display the product price directly.

No size selection.

---

## Quantity

Include

− 1 +

Default quantity is 1.

Update the subtotal live.

---

## Add to Cart

After confirmation

- Add the configured product to the existing cart.
- Close the panel.
- Show a small "Added to Cart" confirmation.
- Update the cart badge immediately.

---

## Closing

Allow closing by

- Close button
- Clicking outside
- ESC key (desktop)

---

## Mobile

The panel should slide up from the bottom.

Desktop

Display as a centered modal.
