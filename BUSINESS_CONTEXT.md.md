## Business Goal

Create a premium online presence that:

- Increases food orders
- Builds customer trust
- Strengthens the MDEE'S brand
- Encourages repeat customers
- Makes ordering quick and effortless

- Burger Lovers

---

## Brand Personality

The website should feel:

- Modern
- Friendly
- Energetic
- Fresh
- Premium
- Family Friendly
- Fast
- Approachable

Avoid:

- Generic
- Corporate
- Luxury fine dining
- Cluttered layouts
- Template designs
- AI-generated appearance

---

## Design Direction

Inspired by modern fast-food brands.

Prioritize:

- Strong visual hierarchy
- Large food photography
- Bright, inviting layouts
- Rounded UI elements
- Spacious sections
- Clear call-to-actions
- Smooth micro-interactions

Design should encourage visitors to order within a few seconds.

Emphasize:

- Fresh Ingredients
- Fast Delivery
- Affordable Prices
- Late-night Service
- Popular Dishes
- Customer Reviews

## Success Criteria

Visitors should immediately understand:
